About Bubble Tones

Bubble tones is a cutomizable generative music program that aims to produce pleasing, unpredictable, elevator-quality tones.

When two bubbles collide, the sum of their tone frequencies plays. When a bubble collides with a larger circle, the difference of their tone frequencies play.

This is an experiment by Chris Schnaars. Find the code here. Please reach out if you notice something not working or to say hello.
